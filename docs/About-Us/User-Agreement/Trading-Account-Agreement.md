## Definitions

**Trading Account**: A fund custody account opened by users on ExinOne for depositing, trading digital assets, and obtaining asset dividends.
**EPC**: ExinOne's platform commission point card, used for transaction fee deductions.
Asset Dividends: Numerical values calculated based on the storage amount of user assets and other factors, used for ExinOne's lending services.

**Asset Custody**

- **Custody Subject**: Digital assets deposited by users into the trading account.
  Custody Duration: From the effective date of the agreement until the assets are completely withdrawn or the account is retired.
- **Custody Fee**: The service is provided free of charge.
- **Custody Interest**: The trading account is not a financial product and does not generate interest.
- **Custody Method**: Only tokens credited to the trading account (OP, SEI, ORDI, ENS, etc.) are stored in the exchange, and freely transferable tokens (BTC, ETH, BOX, MOB, EOS, etc.) are stored within the Mixin Network.
